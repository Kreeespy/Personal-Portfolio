ABOUT ME

My path is defined by a lifelong ambition: to provide decentralized energy solutions that help society take its next great leap forward. Growing up in Indonesia, I viewed the landscape through a lens of both love and critical observation, witnessing how pollution and poverty were often symptoms of a deeper infrastructure gap. I saw that the slow pace of digitalization was leaving rural communities behind, depriving them of the facilities needed to propel themselves forward. I realized that energy is the heartbeat of connection. This realization shaped my life’s goal, not just to build infrastructure, but to create systems that bridge gaps and empower communities to grow. I am dedicated to building a future where technology pushes the world forward, starting from the ground up.

At my core, I am a creator who thrives in the space where calculated logic meets expressive art. I hold a philosophy that creates a standard for everything I do, and to treat every project, no matter how technical, as a form of art. I have a deep appreciation for high-fidelity aesthetics and the intricate details that make a creation truly stand out. I am happiest when I am tackling challenges that demand the full engagement of both sides of my brain, applying a logical mind to solve complex structural problems while using a creative eye to ensure the result is universally beautiful. Whether it is through code or design, I strive for that perfect balance of function and expression, ensuring the work is not just effective, but inspiring.
